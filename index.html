Simi Valley, California, USA

Location

Simi Valley is located 40 miles northwest of downtown Los Angeles.

About

Climate

Simi Valley has a warm and dry climate during the summer with a mean temperature in the 70s; while in the winter, the temperatures tend to be in the cool 50s.

Population

The population of Simi Valley is 125,613.

Notables in Pop Culture

Given its close proximity to Hollywood, Simi Valley and its surrounding hills have been the site of several television shows including Gunsmoke, M*A*S*H, and Little House on the Prairie.

Things To Do

  • Hiking
  • Mountain Biking
  • Touring the Ronald Reagan Presidential Library
